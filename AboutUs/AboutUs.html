ABOUT US

Greetings, fellow enthusiasts! Buckle up for an exhilarating journey with Team 9033 the Ocebots, proudly representing Yorktown High School in Arlington. Established in 2022 with just 10 dedicated students, our team has evolved and grown, and were now a force to be reckoned with, boasting a roster of around 30 passionate members. From our humble beginnings, the Ocebots have surged forward, leaving a trail of success in our wake. In our inaugural year, we demonstrated our dedication and prowess by securing five awards, a testament to the collaborative spirit and hard work of our initial team of 10.

As we represent Yorktown High School in Arlington, the Ocebots are not just a robotics team; were a thriving community of learners and innovators. Our team has become a beacon for STEM education, inspiring students to explore the fascinating world of robotics. The growth of our team mirrors the expanding horizons of our ambitions. What started with 10 students has blossomed into a diverse group of around 30 individuals, each contributing their unique skills and perspectives. Together, weve cultivated an environment that fosters creativity, teamwork, and a passion for pushing the boundaries of whats possible. Our journey is marked by constant evolution, and we invite you to be part of this exciting adventure. The Ocebots are not just about robots; were about forging connections, sparking curiosity, and making an impact in the realm of STEM education. Join us as we navigate the seas of innovation, from our 10-strong beginnings to a formidable team of around 30. Team 9033 Ocebots from Yorktown High School invites you to share in our passion and contribute to the exciting future were building together!
